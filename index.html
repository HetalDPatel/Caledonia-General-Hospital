Anaesthesiology

Department of Anaesthesiology at Ruby General Hospital has state of the art equipments and experienced Team of competent anaesthesiologists who can handle surgeries of various types like cardiac surgeries, cancer surgeries, laparocsopic surgeries, general and gynaecological surgeries, all types of orthopaedic surgeries including joint replacements, trauma surgery including oral and maxillo-facial surgeries.

Emergency & Trauma Care

Ruby General Hospital has a full-fledged emergency department.Largest ER in Kolkata with 30 beds. We are also the leading Trauma Center of Kolkata for the last 25 years as we have treated the maximum number of road traffic accidents due to our location at the junction of the 2 largest and busiest expressways (E M Bypass and Rashbehari Connector) in Kolkata. Ruby also has the reputation of being one of the fastest responding hospitals in an emergency.

Critical Care

Department of Anaesthesiology at Ruby General Hospital has state of the art equipments and experienced Team of competent anaesthesiologists who can handle surgeries of various types like cardiac surgeries, cancer surgeries, laparocsopic surgeries, general and gynaecological surgeries, all types of orthopaedic surgeries including joint replacements, trauma surgery including oral and maxillo-facial surgeries.

Cardiac Care

The department provides a wide range of services starting from diagnostic investigations such as E.C.G., Echocardiogram, Treadmill Test (T.M.T.), Doppler studies, Holter Monitoring etc., to complex treatment procedures. It is equipped with the latest gadgets from world leaders like Hewlett Packard, Marks, Schiller, E-Merck and Beckmann.

Pulmonology / Chest Medicine

Ruby General Hospital has an advanced dedicated Pulmonology department providing Comprehensive, Consultative, Diagnostic and Therapeutic Services under one roof. It is a unit designed for prevention & early detection of respiratory diseases, performing required Interventions and providing Pulmonary Rehabilitation to help patients with respiratory illnesses to lead healthier and productive lives.

Advanced Laparoscopy And GI Surgery

Department of Advanced Laparoscopic & GI Surgery deals with all the benign and malignant Gastro-Intestinal problems. All diagnostic & therapeutic facilities are available under one roof. We aim to provide Minimally invasive treatment to all GI problems, so that patient can get to normal life rapidly after Surgery
